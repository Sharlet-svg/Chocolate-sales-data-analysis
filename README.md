# Chocolate-sales-data-analysis

#Overview
analysis of the various categories and brands of chocolate sold in the year 2023- 2024

#Data source.
The data set was obtained from kaggle
[Download here](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ssssws/chocolate-sales-dataset-2023-2024?resource=download)

#Objectives
i)To determine the best selling brand
ii)To understand the market across different countries.
iii)To determine the trends in profit month by month

#Key insights
BRAND PERFORMANCE
The best selling brand in order of their ranks: **Ferrero**,Cadbury,lint,mars,Godiva,Hershey. The least performing chocolate sales,is the **unbranded one**.
GEOGRAPHICAL ANALYSIS
Looking at countries, The biggest market is in **Canada(19.95%)**,followed by UK(18.93%),US(17.04%),France(17.03%),Australia(15.05%) and **Germany has the least market share at(12.01%)**
TEMPORAL TRENDS
During the fisrt quarter the profit is relatively high at (8.55%), then there's a sharp decline to (7.76%),and finally we end the first quarter with a slight rise to (8.51%). In the second quarter the profit slightly declines to (8.17%) and then slightly increases to (8.48%) in the month of May, and it ends by reducing to (8.18%).
In the third quarter there's an increase in profit to (8.51%),which further increases by 0.01% and then there's a slight reduction to (8.27%). in the final quarter we begin with a slight increase in profit to (8.43%),which then declines to (8.16%) and then there's a slight improvement to (8.46%).
Overally,the profit which we began with in **January(8.55%)** has declined to **(8.46%)** at the end of the year
